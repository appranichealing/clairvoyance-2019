Frequently Asked Questions

REGISTRATIONS and PAYMENTS

Q: Can I come directly to the venue and register?
Sorry, registrations are now closed.

Q: If I am unable to attend the event, after having registered by paying the minimum amount, will I get a refund?
A: The minimum amount paid for registration is Non-Refundable, Non-Transferable (to an event or person). We would consider it as a donation.

ID PROOFS

Q: Is PAN Card acceptable as an ID proof?
A: No. Resort doesn’t accept this. PAN isn't an address proof. It's only photo ID proof.

Q: What are the other ID proofs accepted at resort, than AADHAR?
A: Driving license, Passport.

CLAIRVOYANCE 2019

Q: What are the timings of the event?
A: On 14th December, 2019 - Registration starts from 7:30AM to 9:30AM. Event starts at 9:30AM.

Q: What is my seat number? Is it on a first-come-first-serve basis?
A: Seats will be assigned based on your first payment date, on a first come-first served basis. So, the sooner you have made the initial payment, the earlier is the seat number assigned. Please visit the attendance desk between 7:30AM to 9:30AM to collect your seat number. Note that Badges will only be provided if you have visited the Attendance desk first.

Q: Will there be any books and CDs on sale?
A: Yes, we would have a stall for selling recommended Theosophical books and books of similar interest. There would be some CDs too.

EVENT HALL ETIQUETTES

Q: Is mobile phone allowed inside the Event Hall?
Mobile phones and any other electronic devices, for example MP3 recorders etc. are strictly not allowed inside the Event hall. Participants are requested to leave their mobile phones in their room. We will not be able to provide any locker facilities.

Q: Is photography or videography allowed inside the event hall?
A: Participants are strictly not allowed to take photos or videos or even to make any audio recording during any of the sessions. Please follow the virtue of "Non-Stealing".

RESORT FACILITIES

Q: Is any ATM available at the resort?
A: There is a Union Bank ATM at the Venue (at main gate entrance of Leonia Resorts). Next closest one is after 4 Kms from the resort. We recommend though, you carry sufficient cash, should you need any, for purchases.

Q: Is there any pharmacy at the venue, for any emergency needs?
A: Though we recommend that you carry any medication you are taking regularly, the nearest is Apollo pharmacy, nearly 5 Kms. (at Lothukunta) in case you need one.
There is first-aid available at the Resort and with us, should you need one. Please contact any of the volunteers for the following items like Volini spray, Sanitary napkins, Band aids, generic medicins like Paracetemol, Combiflam, etc.

Q: Are there any transport arrangements from the resort to the airport?
A: For a drop to Airport, you may leave your information at the Foundation stall. We will facilitate finding if someone else is going in that direction at your time. You may pool. Alternately, you may book a cab in advance.

Q: What are the options to reach the Venue from the airport?
Participants who are coming from outside Hyderabad by flight, you can explore one of the below cab options from Airport.
App based / low fare options:

  • Uber
  • Ola
Note: Both cab operators accept CASH as option. Before confirming the booking, choose the payment method as Cash or Debit/Credit card in the app.
High fare options:
  • Meru
  • Sky Cabs
There will also be pre-paid government cabs or private cabs available at airport that you can check out (counters are inside the arrival hall, not accessible once you exit the arrival area)

Q: What are the nearby railway stations?
Hyderabad has 4 main rail stations:

  • Secunderabad - distance to Leonia - approx 24-28kms
  • Nampally - distance to Leonia - approx 30-35kms
  • Kacheguda - distance to Leonia - approx 30-32kms
  • Begumpet - distance to Leonia - approx 27-30kms
The best and nearest station to venue is Secunderabad bus station as well as Secunderabad railway station for those who opt train.

Q: What are the options to reach the Venue by bus or any other transports?

If coming by bus
If coming by bus, there are Major bus stops such as MGBS, CBS, NAMPALLY, UPPAL, MEHDIPATNAM, SECUNDERABAD etc.
Options for reaching venue from Secunderabad station
  • Bus no. 211 will be dropping you at outer ring road, fare would be around ₹20 - from there you will have to take another mode of travel
  • Direct Auto will be available easily, price ranging from ₹250 to ₹350 depending on time (and your negotiations skills)
  • Cabs are also available from Secunderabad for around ₹450 to ₹500 depending on the demand. Book through known apps like Uber, Ola, Yellow cabs
Options for reaching destination from Hyderabad - MGBS Bus Depot
utos and cabs are available at any time, Fare could be around ₹350 to ₹450 for auto and ₹550 to ₹650 for cab
Cabs and autos are available from all other stations as well
Note: Fares are for indicative purposes only. They may vary.

Q: Is there a child-care facility at the resort?
A: There is no child-care facility at the resort. Participants coming with small children would need to take care of them on their own. Foundation would not be responsible for the same.
